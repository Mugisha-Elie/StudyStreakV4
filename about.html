About StudyStreak

StudyStreak is your personal productivity companion designed to help students and lifelong learners maintain a consistent study habit. With tools like streak tracking, file uploads, progress visualization, and more, we make staying on top of your goals rewarding and motivating.

  • 📅 Track your daily study streaks
  • 📈 View your study progress and milestones
  • 📤 Upload and organize your learning materials
  • 🔐 Secure login/signup using Firebase Authentication
  • 📊 Easily view your file history and study timeline

Whether you're a student, a self-learner, or just trying to stay consistent — StudyStreak is here to help you build strong habits, one day at a time.
